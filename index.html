Kid Chameleon

Introduction

Kid Chameleon is just a regular boy but he can pop on different character outfits to help him defeat baddies. Each character outfit can enhance Kid Chameleon's abilities but with too much damage, Kid Chameleon will just turn into a regular boy again.

Background

Kid Chameleon is a great old school 90s Sega Megadrive platform game. Here is a link for more information on the game: Kid Chameleon Wiki or Kid Chameleon Wiki (new tab).
